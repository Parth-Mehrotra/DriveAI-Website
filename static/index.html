Where are we now?

We are getting ready to map out 3D space with recently purchased sensors and test our algorithms. There are efforts in Princeton University overseen by Professor Kornhauser and in Rutgers University led by DriveAI and derivative/d. Encourage your Assembly Person to pass NJ Assembly Bill A1326!


We Need Your Support

There is no legislation for this amazing technology yet. We can change that and are close. New Jersey State Senate passed the bill unanimously. The next step is to have it passed by Assembly. You can help by either emailing or phoning your local Assembly Person. Assembly people tell us every voice of support inspires them, usually they hear complaints!


Moving Forward

We have a car on the way and will have a demo ready by the summertime. We need your help getting through Assembly.
